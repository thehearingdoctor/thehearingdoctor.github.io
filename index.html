About Me

For 15 years, Dr. Araya has provided audiological services in Upstate New York. He has worked in a clinical setting with a multi-partner Ear, Nose and Throat practice, providing diagnostic evaluations on pediatric to geriatric populations. He has been in private practice for the past eight years. His Amsterdam practice is locally owned and operated.
