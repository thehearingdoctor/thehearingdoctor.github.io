About Me

Dr. Araya has serviced Upstate New York's audiological needs for over 20 years. Prior to starting his own practice, Dr. Araya worked in a clinical, multi-partner, Ear, Nose, and Throat practice while providing diagnostic evaluations for the pediatric to geriatric populations. As an Amsterdam native, he opened his first privately-owned location in 2008 and has since expanded to service customers in Gloversville.
